About

I'm Akshay Manglik, an Applied AI Engineer at Scale AI.

I graduated from Columbia University in 2025 with a B.A. in Computer Science and Economics, and a minor in Mathematics.

Dereferenced is my blog about the intersection of technology, economics, and philosophy. I'm especially interested in understanding complex systems from the ground up, whether those be market systems, technological systems, or systems of ideas. Feel free to reach out to chat or with feedback on my writing!
